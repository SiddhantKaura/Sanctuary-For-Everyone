About Us

Sanctuary for everyone is an initiative taken by us wholly for the Welfare of pets and their owners. We work together to connect working pet owners and people who would be voluntarily willing to take care of their pets for some time. We aim to provide a safe space to pets when their owners are out working and give them a second home. We want to build a community where neither the pets feel lonely nor do their owners live with guilt for leaving their pets alone for long.
